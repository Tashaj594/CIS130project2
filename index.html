About

Name: Ari Patrinos

Running for: City Controller

Party: Republican

Northwest Philadelphia native Ari Patrinos is a Harvard graduate with a masters in political science from the University of Chicago and experience as a Wall Street stockbroker. He is running to help the City provide basic services like clean streets and quality public schools, and to recruit highly qualified workers while addressing tax policy.
